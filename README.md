## 🧑‍💻 About Me

My journey into technology began years ago when I took my very first course in **Computer Assembly and Maintenance**, sparking a lifelong curiosity for how things work under the hood. 

Later, I pursued my passion for human performance, earning a Bachelor's, Licentiate, and a **Post-Graduate degree in Exercise Physiology**. During my academic years, I deeply engaged in **scientific research**, participating in study groups and **publishing a peer-reviewed scientific paper**. This background trained me to think critically, analyze complex data, and approach problem-solving with rigorous methodology.

Driven by a vision to blend **health, well-being, and technology**, I am transitioning into a **Full Stack Web Developer**. My professional path also includes experience as a **Marketing Manager** (learning web management and WordPress entirely through self-directed study), which taught me to build solutions with a strong focus on business goals and user behavior.

I am actively mastering software architecture, practicing **English daily**, and preparing to build high-impact global solutions.
